Signup Template Steps

  1. Reuse your shared validation helpers from forms.js instead of rewriting field validation here.
  2. Validate displayName, username, and password first.
  3. After field validation passes, call findUser(username) from store.js.
  4. If the username already exists, show Username already taken. on the username field.
  5. If the username is new, call addUser(displayName, username, password) and redirect to login.html.
